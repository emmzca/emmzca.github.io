Backstory

Thor Odinson is from a planet called Asgard, he is the son of the king and has an adopted brother named Loki, who is from another planet named Jotunheim, who has fought against Asgard. While Thor is known as the 'god of thunder, Loki is known as the 'god of mischeive'. They end up having a very complicated relationship, as many betrayals happen between them. His father Odin, raised them together without telling them about Loki till he found out himself when battling on Jotunheim with Asgardians. Thor was about to become King when the Jotunheims attacked Asgard and Thor went to fight against them, betraying his father. This caused him to be banished to Earth. He learns about the different customs of Earth, as life on Asgard is very different and more royal and glamorous. This is how he becomes known on Earth, as he has abilities greater than humans, such as being a lot stronger. Apart from that he is known as the 'god of thunder' which is why he also has the ability to control lighting. He is also worthy of the Mjolnir which is a hammer that can only be wielded by someone who is 'worthy' of it. So, practically the only person who can carry it is Thor.

Powers and Abilities

As the 'god of thunder', Thor is able to summon lightning and thunder, as well as wielding the Mjolnir, which was made from the heart of a dying star. It has the strength to destroy many things, as well as channeling lighting and power. It also allows Thor to fly as well. Since it is only to be held by someone 'worthy', if it's placed or held by anyone else it would them to not be able to move from it because they are not 'worthy' to hold or move it.

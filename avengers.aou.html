About

Avengers: Age of Ultron is the sequel of The Avengers. The 'world's mightiest heroes' reunite to fight against an artificial intelligent robot that was created because of Tony Stark. The AI, Unltron, wants to destroy the world as well as 'extinct' The Avengers. In this movie, The Avengers meet twins Wanda and Pietro Maximoff, both successful Hydra science experiments, who originally fought against them. Wanda has telekenesis, telepathy and energy manipulation. While her older twin brother has super speed. However, they are not the villians in this movie, despite fighting against the Avengers, as they ally with them later on. Vision is also born in this movie, he is Tony's JARVIS AI system in a vibranium body, he allies with the heroes too.

Significant Items

Mind Stone

The Mind Stone is one of the infinity stones (one of the six , where each of them was a singularity that existed before the universe was created). It appears in this movie as the power of Vision but has also appeared in The Avengers, inside the scepter that Loki uses. This stone provides a high level of mental abilities, being able to manipulate other minds as well as a powerful amount of intelligence. This allows Vision to be omnipotent. In Loki's scepter, it was used to manipulate others to his will and for the power to use in battles.

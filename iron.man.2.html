About

Iron Man 2 talks about Tony's battle against a villain named 'whiplash' who has used Tony's own technology to build a energy powered whip, as well as a larger version of a iron man suit. Apart from battling him, Tony also has to deal with palladium poisoning from his old version of the arc reactor in his chest, he develops a new element to solve this problem. This movie is also the one that first introdues 'the avengers initiative', Tony being the first one to know about this out of the group, apart from Natasha Romanoff (Black Widow) who is also in this movie and is working for Nick Fury. This film was critiqued as one of the less enjoyable Marvel movie, but the room for improvement in this movie was taken for the last movie of the trilogy, as it was a lot more heartfelt as it discusses more about Tony rather than just Iron Man battles. However, none of the movies in this trilogy compares to the first one as it was more critically acclaimed but also the one that started it all.

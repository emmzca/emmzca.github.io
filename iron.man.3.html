About

Iron Man 3 shows Tony's battle with anxiety after the scarring events of The Avengers, as well as his battle against Aldrich Killian. He was interested in working with Tony a very long time ago but was rejected by him, but since then he has advanced his technology project a lot more and has been conducting experiments of regenerating tissue. However, a glitch in this experiment is that if the subject is unstable, they will burst into flames and explode. His first battle was against a terrorist that was going by the name 'the mandarin', who has orchestrated many bombings but with no evidence to find how he managed to do it. The two: Killian and the mandarin are connected in this movie that is somewhat of a plot twist. Tony also developed a new Iron Man suit (Mark 42) in the beginning that could sense his location and was helpful escaping a capture later on in the movie.

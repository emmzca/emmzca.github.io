About

The Avengers talks about 'the world's mightiest heroes' (Captain America, Thor Odinson, Iron Man, The Hulk, Black Widow and Hawkeye) assembling to fight against an evil army of aliens called Chitauri that was led by Thor's brother, Loki. This group of heroes, The Avengers, was assembled as 'the avengers initiative' that was made by Nick Fury of Shield. The film is set in New York and starts when the tesseract, also known as the container for the space stone (one of the six infinity stones, where each of them was a singularity that existed before the universe was created) is activated in a Shield lab. It became the highest grossing film of 2012 and was the third highest in the world.

Significant Items

Tesseract

The Tesseract was the power that opened a portal to space that allowed Loki to bring his army of the Chitauri. It was also the weapon that was used in Captain America: The First Avenger. It is also the container for the space stone which holds an infinite large amount of energy. It was first placed in Odin's throne, then retrieved by the villian of Captain America: The First Avenger (Red Skull), and later contained by Howard Stark. It was later retrieved by the organization, Shield but stolen by Loki as the event of The Avengers but returned to Asgard after the events.

About

Thor, being the son of Odin, is supposed to inherit the throne of Asgard from his aging father. However, on the day that he is to be crowned, Asgard's enemy from Jothumheim attacked the palace. After the one Frost Giant from there that attacked was defeated, Thor decides to travel to Jothumheim to get revenge, despite not being allowed to from his father. This is why he is banished by Odin to Earth, till he proves his worthiness to be king. Thor's brother, Loki tries to take over the throne thoughout Thor's banishment. The movie also follows Thor's journey throug a small town on Earth, trying to get back to Asgard. The Mjolnir is a weapon that appears in this movie, it is only to be held and used by the one who is worthy. This item was banished to Earth with Thor and only when he proved his worthiness, he was able to return home. This movie also shows how Earth knows about Thor, foreshadowing his return to help with battles on Earth to keep peace.

Significant Items

Casket of Ancient Winters

The Casket of Ancient Winters was a relic and weapon that once belonged to the Frost Giants of Jotunheim who used it to vanquish enemy armies and conquer enemy realms. It is capable of producing and projecting an infinite icy wind that would freeze enemies and plunge a realm into an Ice Age. It was captured by the Einherjar of Asgard and sealed in Odin's Vault before being stolen by Loki. When Loki held it in the events of the movie, it revealed his true identity of being the son of a Frost Giant.
